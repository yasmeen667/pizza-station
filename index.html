About Pizza Station


Since 2001

Pizza Station is known as the first New-Yorker pizzeria in Egypt as well as the first place to ever sell pizza by slice since 2001. At Pizza Station, we don’t just make pizza. We make people happy.

Pizza station was built on the belief that pizza night should be special, and we carry that belief into everything we do. With more than 60 years of experience under our belts, we understand how to best serve our customers through tried and true service principles: We create food we’re proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.
